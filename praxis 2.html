// About

Uncertainty is not a fatality. It is a variable to model.

PRAXIS was born from a simple observation: defence decision-makers lack tools capable of simultaneously integrating geopolitical, industrial, and logistical dimensions.

We believe an informed decision is one that has seen all its scenarios. PRAXIS gives strategists the ability to simulate the future before committing to it.

02 — Vision

Why PRAXIS?

In a world where crises accelerate and overlap, traditional tools analyse one dimension at a time and look to the past.

PRAXIS models the interconnection of crises to produce forward-looking, quantified, actionable recommendations. Our goal: to become the standard for strategic defence decision support in Europe by 2027.
